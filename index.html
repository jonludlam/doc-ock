Library doc-ock

This library defines the AST used in by odoc to represent documentation. It also implements the operations working on this AST to do:

  • the resolving of paths and references
  • the expansion of signatures, i.e. it performs substitutions, etc.

People wanting to process .odoc files (eg. to add a new backend) should only need to look at DocOck.Types and DocOck.Paths.

For everything else, DocOck is the main entry point of the library.
